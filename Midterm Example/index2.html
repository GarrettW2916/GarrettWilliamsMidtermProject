North Carolina Tarheels Basketball

History

Frank McGuire led the team to its first NCAA championship in 1957. Then coach Dean Smith eventually took over and established the program as a powerhouse in college basketball. The Tar Heels secured two national championships under Smith and have won the national championships three times under current coach Roy Williams.

Achievments

Out of its 20 Final Four appearances, the Tar Heels have won the national title six times. Three of UNC's titles were won under head coach Roy Williams, most recently in 2017 against Gonzaga. North Carolina was playing in its second consecutive title game, after losing to Villanova at the buzzer the previous year.
